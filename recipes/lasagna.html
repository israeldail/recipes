Lasagna

Description

This is a cheesy lasagna with a meat-sauce base.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Pasta
  • Meat-sauce

Steps

  1. Layer Pasta
  2. Put sauce in between layers
  3. Top off with cheese (personal preference)
  4. Pre-heat oven to 400 F
  5. Let cook for 1 hour
  6. Slice and enjoy :)
